About Us

{{ site.name }} is a full service professional Land Surveying firm providing personalized “hands-on” professional services to private and public clients in the residential, commercial, industrial, institutional and municipal sectors from its office headquarters in Manchester, Connecticut and its satellite office in Putnam, Connecticut.

Extensive experience in land use planning and development has given the Firm excellent working expertise and knowledge in the areas of planning, zoning, site development, wetlands and drainage, environmental regulation, permitting and siting issues. A long-term continuing working relationship with a variety of local, State and Federal regulatory and support agencies and personnel enables the Firm to keep abreast of continually changing land use rules and regulations and development practices in order to meet the needs of each client on an individual basis, and represent them professionally in all matters before the various land use review and regulatory agencies and staff which may be necessary for their projects.

{{ site.name }} is headed by Robert R. Messier, C.E., L.S., President and Managing Principal. The Firm is licensed by the State Department of Consumer Protection for corporate surveying and engineering practice in the State of Connecticut under License No. 0022, and is fully insured for general liability, automobile liability, worker's compensation and employers' liability, and professional liability.
